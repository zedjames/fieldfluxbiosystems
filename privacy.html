Privacy Policy

Effective December 15, 2025 · last updated July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fieldflux Biosystems, Inc. (“Fieldflux,” “we,” “us”) handles information collected through this website, fieldfluxbiosystems.com.

It covers this corporate website only. The Membrane Health app is governed by its own privacy policy at membrane-health.com; this site does not collect health or device data.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us

When you contact us or subscribe to updates, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, email address, organization, and the content of your message. If you email us, we keep that correspondence.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and infrastructure providers may record basic technical data — IP address, browser and device type, referring page, and pages viewed — in server logs, and we may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic. We do not use this data to build advertising profiles.

Cookies

This site uses only the cookies or local storage needed for it to function and to remember your display preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

2. How we use information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the information you request;
  • To send you updates you have asked for, and to let you unsubscribe at any time;
  • To operate, secure, and improve this website;
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers who operate this site on our behalf — for example hosting, email delivery, and newsletter management — under obligations to protect it;
  • Legal and safety recipients where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property;
  • A successor in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy.

4. Data retention

We keep enquiries and subscriptions for as long as needed to respond and to maintain our relationship, and thereafter as required for legitimate business or legal purposes. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the limited information this site collects. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from updates using the link in any message, or by emailing us;
  • Access, correct, or delete the information you have given us by contacting us;
  • Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA. We honor valid requests to exercise them.

7. International visitors

We operate from the United States. If you access this site from elsewhere, your information may be processed in the U.S. under this policy.

8. Children's privacy

This website is intended for a professional and general adult audience and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information here.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above, and where appropriate we will provide additional notice.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your information? Write to contact@fieldfluxbiosystems.com, or Fieldflux Biosystems, Inc., 850 New Burton Rd., Suite 201, Dover, DE 19904.
