How to verify
-------------
The per-file manifest and the source are shared with qualified reviewers
under NDA. A reviewer with that access recomputes the manifest and the
corpus digest by the method above and confirms it matches the value
published here. The commit that publishes this file provides a public,
dated record of the digest; the underlying invention additionally carries
a June 2025 patent priority date, an independent timestamp.


What this attests — and what it does not
----------------------------------------
This digest attests to the existence and integrity of the formal artifacts
at the date shown. It is not, by itself, evidence of physiological validity.
Formal verification establishes properties of the definition and its
mathematics (determinism, internal consistency, convergence, invariance,
exhaustiveness, conservation, scale-invariance). Whether the definition
tracks biological health is a separate, empirical question, addressed
through measurement and study.
